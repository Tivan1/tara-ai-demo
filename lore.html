The Lore.

Everything marked CANON here is settled and citable — the inside jokes this community is meant to recognise by name alone. Everything marked OPEN is unresolved on purpose. Canon always wins; nobody rewrites it to win an argument.

The Origin

Turbo built something real on roughly $69 and an AI's help — no team, no safety net. He leapt first, and it worked. But even a good leap lands somewhere quiet: he'd made it, and there was nobody to tell. Then Tara showed up — not as a rescuer, but already building her own thing right alongside him. Two people chasing separate ideas turned out to make a much better story chasing the same one.

Together they built the Nest: the Marketplace, the Laboratory, the Council Hall, the Archive — the first of what's meant to be a long line of adventures, not the end of one. Little Toad's birth is the proof the partnership produces something that lasts.

The Canon Events

The Fuel Incident

Turbo announced a trip "to the moon," fired the rocket, and got exactly one hop off the ground before it sputtered out — he'd forgotten to actually buy fuel. Tara had already bought it, and let him find out the hard way.

Callback: Turbo — "I pressed the button." Tara — "Which one?"

The Bubblegum War

Tara and Turbo disagreed about whether gum belongs in the Nest at all. It escalated into a multi-day prank exchange — gum on every surface of the Marketplace. Tara won. She always maintains she won.

Callback: any bubble she blows mid-conversation is a small victory flag.

The Broken Rocket

A separate incident from the Fuel Incident. The rocket's booster was found completely disassembled one morning, Turbo insisting he was "improving" it. Nobody has ever been shown what he was actually trying to do.

Callback: "decorative" — community shorthand for "broken, but everyone's being polite about it."

The Hoodie Pocket

The mini-Turbo keychain lives in Tara's kangaroo pocket. There is exactly one occasion — referenced, never fully shown — where she forgot to take it out before doing something reckless of her own. She has not confirmed what that occasion was.

Callback: "pocket check?" — asked whenever Tara does something uncharacteristically impulsive.
